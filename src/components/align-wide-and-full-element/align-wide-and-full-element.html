对齐宽元素和全元素

你可以添加 uix-alignfull 到 HTML 标签或图像标签，以溢出容器的安全宽度。 

<div class="uix-alignfull" style="border: 3px dotted #000000; padding: 50px 0;margin-bottom: 50px;">
    <img src="assets/images/demo/cool-slider-1.jpg" alt="big image" width="1024" height="682">
</div>

<div style="border: 3px dotted #000000; padding: 50px 0;margin-bottom: 50px;">
    <img class="uix-alignfull" src="assets/images/demo/cool-slider-2.jpg" alt="big image" width="1024" height="682">
</div>
